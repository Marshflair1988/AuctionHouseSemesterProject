About BidHive Auction House

Our Mission

At BidHive Auction House, our mission is to create a trusted, user-friendly platform where students from Noroff School of Technology can buy and sell items through an exciting auction format. We believe in connecting buyers with unique items while helping sellers get the best value for their offerings.

Whether you're looking to find rare collectibles, vintage items, electronics, or handmade crafts, BidHive provides a secure environment for discovering treasures and engaging in fair bidding competitions.

How It Works

Register

Create an account using your @stud.noroff.no email. Set up your profile with a profile picture and start with 1,000 credits.

Bid on Items

Browse active listings and place bids on items that interest you. Keep track of your bids and get notified when you're outbid.

Sell Items

Create listings for items you want to sell. Add compelling descriptions, images, and tags to attract more bidders.

When an auction ends, the highest bidder wins the item. Credits are transferred from the buyer to the seller, and both parties receive notifications about the successful transaction.
